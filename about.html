Who am I?

I'm Jared! I am an AWS DevSecOps developer with four years of experience, and three years spent in a sysadmin and security role. I really enjoy automating manual processes, and creating pipelines and infrastructure from scratch.

In my spare time I enjoy racing my car, playing jazz trombone, and many outdoor activities. I especially enjoy camping in the summer, and skating on the Rideau Canal in the winter.

What Have I Done?

I have worked on many projects, but these are the ones I am most proud of.

Autocross timer: a website for autocross live timing, built with a VueJS frontend and Go backend. I am building this for my local motorsports club, MCO. It is currently in development, but building and deploying is fully automated.

Assassins.TECH: a twist on the classic game Assassin using SMS text messaging, built entirely in Bash. It was made by Kyle Farwell and I, and it won 2nd place at the THacks 2 Hackathon.
